Call for Contributions

We invite researchers, practitioners, and students to contribute to our workshop on agency and trust in robotic avatars. This is an opportunity to share cutting-edge research and engage with a multidisciplinary community.

Types of Contributions

Research Papers

Format: 4-6 pages IEEE format
Scope: Original research on agency, trust, and avatar control systems

  • Experimental studies on sense of agency
  • Novel avatar control interfaces
  • Trust measurement and enhancement
  • Cognitive modeling of human-robot interaction

Position Papers

Format: 2-4 pages IEEE format
Scope: Perspectives on future directions and challenges

  • Ethical implications of robotic avatars
  • Design principles for agency-preserving systems
  • Multidisciplinary research challenges
  • Technology roadmaps and visions

Poster Presentations

Format: 2-page extended abstract + poster
Scope: Work-in-progress and preliminary results

  • Ongoing research projects
  • System demonstrations
  • Preliminary experimental findings
  • Student research projects

Demonstration Proposals

Format: 2-page demo description
Scope: Interactive system demonstrations

  • Avatar control platforms
  • Agency measurement tools
  • Trust assessment systems
  • Novel interaction paradigms

Topics of Interest

We welcome contributions addressing any aspect of agency and trust in robotic avatars, including but not limited to:

Technical Topics

  • Sense of agency measurement and modeling
  • Latency compensation in avatar control
  • Multimodal feedback systems
  • AI-assisted avatar control
  • Real-time synchronization techniques
  • Haptic and force feedback integration
  • Virtual and augmented reality interfaces
  • Distributed avatar control systems

Human Factors Topics

  • User experience in avatar systems
  • Trust building and maintenance
  • Cognitive load and mental models
  • Embodiment and presence
  • Social aspects of avatar interaction
  • Accessibility and inclusion
  • Training and skill acquisition
  • Ethical considerations and implications

Submission Guidelines

  • Template: IEEE Conference format
  • Language: English
  • Submission: PDF format via EasyChair
  • Review Process: Single-blind peer review

Position Papers 2-4 pages Vision, Impact, Clarity 10-minute talk + Q&A
