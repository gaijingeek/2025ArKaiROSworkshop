Workshop Environment

Virtual workshop setup and interactive session environments:

Interactive Session Setup

Our virtual workshop environment enables real-time interaction with robotic avatar systems, allowing participants to experience firsthand how different control paradigms affect sense of agency and trust. The setup includes synchronized multimodal feedback, latency simulation, and collaborative control scenarios.

Research Areas

Visual overview of the multidisciplinary research areas covered in the workshop:

  • Cognitive Neuroscience: Neural mechanisms underlying sense of agency and motor control
  • Human-Computer Interaction: Interface design for intuitive avatar control
  • Robotics Engineering: Technical implementation of avatar control systems
  • Psychology: Trust, acceptance, and user experience in human-robot interaction
  • Machine Learning: AI-assisted control and adaptive interfaces
  • Ethics: Implications of extending human capabilities through robotic avatars
