Technical Sessions

The summer school features expert talks, interactive sessions, and hands-on workshops covering key aspects of reproducible research in robotics.

Session Format

  • Expert Talks: 25 minutes + 5 minutes Q&A
  • Workshops: Hands-on sessions with practical tools
  • Panel Discussions: Interactive discussions with community leaders

Speakers & Topics

Our distinguished speakers bring expertise from academia and industry, covering diverse aspects of reproducible robotics research:

  • Performance Evaluation Methodologies
  • Benchmarking Standards and Frameworks
  • Reproducibility Tools and Platforms
  • Community-driven Validation Processes
  • Case Studies from Medical Robotics
