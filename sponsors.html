Primary Sponsors

MOONSHOT Goal 1

Realization of a society in which human beings can be free from limitations of body, brain, space, and time by 2050

MOONSHOT Goal 1 supports revolutionary research and development toward realizing a society where people can transcend physical limitations through advanced technologies including robotic avatars. This workshop directly contributes to understanding the cognitive foundations necessary for effective human-avatar interaction.

Internet of Brains

Liberation from Biological Limitations via Physical, Cognitive and Perceptual Augmentation

Our goal is to make a world in which anyone can participate in creative social activities regardless of physical or brain limitations by controlling a large number of cybernetic avatars (CAs) in coordination using only the ideas of words and actions that come to mind.

ARAYA
